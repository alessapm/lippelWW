Lippel Woodworking

Hand-crafted, custom masterpieces ranging from cutting board to farm tables and much more. We focus on traditional joinery, modern designs and high quality craftsmanship. We like to carve spoons, chainsaw stumps, plank canoes, keep our chisels sharp with stones, build pinball machines & fine furniture.
